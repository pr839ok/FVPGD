Introduction to guangdong institute of microbiology

Guangdong institute of microbiology, formerly known as the south China institute of fungi of the Chinese academy of sciences, was established on May 19, 1964 with the approval of the national science and technology commission. Relying on the institute, the guangdong microbiological analysis and testing center was established in 1999 with the approval of the guangdong provincial compilation office.
